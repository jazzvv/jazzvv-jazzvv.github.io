What does Sasquatch eat?

According to experts sasquatch is an omnivore that consumes around 5,000 calories daily. The majority of their diet consist of nuts, berries, and some game. Though most witnesses claim Sasquatch always has a bag of beef jerky in hand.

Commonly Used Bait

Foods that are commonly used for bait.

  • Beef Jerky
  • Berries
  • apples
  • seeds
  • Fast Food: KFC, McDonalds, Burger King, etc.
